## Federal Open Market Committee Meeting Minutes
### Title and Authors
The title of the paper is "Minutes of the Federal Open Market Committee" and the authors are the members of the Federal Open Market Committee (FOMC) of the Federal Reserve System.

### Research Question
The research question is not explicitly stated, but the paper aims to discuss the current economic situation, financial market developments, and the FOMC's monetary policy decisions.

### Methodology
The methodology used in the paper is a descriptive analysis of the current economic situation, financial market developments, and the FOMC's monetary policy decisions. The paper is based on the minutes of the FOMC meeting held on March 17-18, 2026.

### Key Findings
Some key findings from the paper include:
* The front-month futures prices for crude oil increased by about 50% over the period.
* The one-year inflation swap rate rose by nearly 50 basis points over the period.
* The probability of rate hikes through the next period increased to about 30%.
* The median of the modal paths reported in the Desk survey continued to show two 25 basis point rate cuts this year.
* Treasury yields ended the intermeeting period higher, on net, more so at the short end.
* Broad equity prices fell about 5% over the period.
* The unemployment rate was 4.4% in February, and the average monthly change in total payroll employment was low over January and February.
* Real GDP growth was solid last year, and available indicators suggested that real GDP growth had picked up in the first quarter of this year.
